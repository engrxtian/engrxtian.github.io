If you're looking for a relaxing and fun-filled getaway for the whole family, look no further than the charming Little Farm Resort, located on the beautiful farm in Sta Maria, Licab, Nueva Ecija. Our cozy and affordable resort is the perfect destination for families looking for sun, and swimming pool without breaking the bank. With over 50 comfortable and spacious rooms and suites, each with a private balcony or patio overlooking the lush gardens or sparkling pool, you'll feel right at home as soon as you arrive. Our friendly and accommodating staff is always on hand to ensure that your stay is enjoyable and stress-free. When you're ready to explore, take a short stroll to the nearby farm, where you can soak up the sun, and swim in the warm swimming pool. Or, if you're feeling adventurous, head out on a fishing or excursion from the marina just a few steps away. Back at the resort, enjoy a refreshing dip in our outdoor pool, challenge the family to a game of mini-golf or shuffleboard, or simply relax on a lounge chair and take in the peaceful surroundings. When hunger strikes, visit our casual onsite restaurant, where you can enjoy fresh seafood, juicy burgers, and delicious salads in a laid-back atmosphere. At Little Farm Resort, we believe that a relaxing and fun-filled vacation shouldn't break the bank. Come see for yourself why families return to our charming and affordable resort.

About Us

Welcome to Angelina's Farm Resort, a charming and affordable resort located in the heart of Licab. Our resort is designed to provide you with a relaxing and enjoyable vacation experience, offering comfortable accommodations and a variety of activities and amenities to suit your needs. With 20 cozy rooms and suites, each with its own unique charm and character, our resort is the perfect destination for families, couples, and solo travelers alike. From our comfortable bedding and complimentary Wi-Fi to our stunning views and convenient amenities, we've thought of everything to make your stay as comfortable and enjoyable as possible. Our resort is home to a variety of activities and amenities, including a biking, fishing area, a relaxing ambient room, and a delicious foods on restaurant. Whether you're looking to relax by the pool, explore the local area, or simply unwind in the comfort of your room, we've got you covered. At Angelina's Farm Resort, we are committed to providing our guests with exceptional service and a warm and welcoming atmosphere. We believe that a memorable vacation doesn't have to break the bank, and we're proud to offer affordable rates and unbeatable value to our guests. Come see for yourself why Angelina's Farm Resort is the perfect destination for your next getaway.
